Become a Mentor

One mentor can make all the difference.

Only 35% of entrepreneurs worldwide are mentoring young people. The EY G20 Entrepreneurship Barometer (2013) indicated that Canada lags behind global peers in coordinated support for youth entrepreneurs, scoring poorly on mentorship, the provision of incubators by businesses and universities, and the availability of networks such as entrepreneur clubs and associations.

We set out to change that. With your help, we can achieve our goal of matching every young Canadian entrepreneur with a mentor that will help them realize their fullest potential. By joining today, you are simultaneously plugging your company into the next generation of innovative talent that can help solve real problems for your organization. When youth and passion meets experience and wisdom, anything becomes possible.
